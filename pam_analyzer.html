What the labels mean
UC1 / UC2 — Up Continuation (long). Up flow, a force-bottom bear-trap at an LP, bullish EXE. "2" = a deeper two-leg pullback.
DC1 / DC2 — Down Continuation (short). Down flow, a force-top bull-trap, bearish EXE. "2" = deeper bounce.
UR1 / UR2 — Up→Down Reversal (short). Up flow, force-top at an LP, bearish EXE. "2" = confirmed by a majority flush-up.
DR1 / DR2 — Down→Up Reversal (long). Down flow, force-bottom, bullish EXE. "2" = after a majority flush-down.
Bull-FS / Bear-FS — Force Strike. A mother/baby inside-bar trap: price false-breaks the inside-bar then reverses with an EXE. Standalone, any flow.
Force top / bottom — price pierces an un-breached swing high/low (LP) then closes back inside it: a failed break = a trap.
EXE bar — the candle traded off: PIN, Ice Cream or Mark up/down. Flush bars are exits, not entries.
1 vs 2 — a depth/flush heuristic here, not exact slide geometry; eyeball the structure to confirm.
